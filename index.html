Welcome to Dam Clicker!

The rules of the game are quite simple.

  1. Click button = point
  2. Clicking button lots = point stonks
  3. Buy upgrades with points to make clicks earn more points
  4. Earn a certain amount of points to level up (levelling up unlocks new upgrades)
  5. Get as many points as you can

Yes, so basically like every other clicker game you played ever.
